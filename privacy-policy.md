Please read this policy carefully before using the App. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information
When you sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- Your Google account email address
- Your Google account display name
- Your Google account profile picture (if available)
- A unique user ID (UID) assigned by Firebase Authentication

### 1.2 Usage & Subscription Data
We collect and store the following in Firebase Firestore:
- Your subscription plan type (Free, Basic, Pro, or Premium)
- Your current credit balance
- Number of videos generated (lifetime count for free users)
- Subscription start and expiry dates
- Whether your subscription is active

### 1.3 Content You Upload
When you use the App to generate videos:
- You upload images that are processed using an AI video generation model (LTX-Video 13B)
- Uploaded images are used solely to generate the requested video
- We do not store, retain, or share your uploaded images beyond the processing period

### 1.4 Device & Log Information
We may automatically collect:
- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- IP address
- Date and time of access
- Crash logs and error reports (for improving the App)

### 1.5 Payment Information
Payments are processed entirely by **Google Play Billing**. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit/debit card details. Google handles all payment processing securely. We only receive confirmation of whether a purchase was successful.
